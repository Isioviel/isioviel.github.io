Robots

Information on my Antweight, Pi Wars & Science Oxford robots

Antweight

Antweights are fighting robots (think Robot Wars but tiny!), a maximum of 150g in weight and small enough to fit inside a 4" cube.

I currently compete with Eyesorasaurus, out-of-micro:trol and Tik-Tok.

Pi Wars

Pi Wars is an annual (ish) robotics competition, where teams compete in a variety of remote control and autonomous challenges.

I competed with Sky Fawkes in 2019, and am entering Townson Family Salvage in 2020.

Science Oxford

Science Oxford is an educational charity, for whom I work as Technology Projects Officer. I have built two different sets of robots to use in workshops.
