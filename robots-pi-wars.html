Pi Wars

Information about my Pi Wars teams

Sky Fawkes (2019)

Team Sky Fawkes was comprised of myself, some colleagues, ex-colleagues and friends.

The theme for Pi Wars 2019 was space, so our robot was shaped like a rocket.

To fit with the name, young people from the computing club I run for Science Oxford designed fireworks to run in a BBC micro:bit attached to the top of our robot.

Read about our progress during the build on the Sky Fawkes blog.

Townson Family Salvage (2020)

Townson Family Salvage are myself and my dad, Paul.

This year's theme is disasters, so our robot will resemble a salvage space ship.

We will be updating our progress on the Townson Salvage blog.
